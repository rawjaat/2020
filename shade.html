D'VAN HOWARD

SHADE

Industrial Design, Home, Office

As one of my projects while studying abroad at Tama Art University in Tokyo, Japan, the goal of the project was to identify a problem and develop a solution. I chose to tackle the problem of smartphone addiction and where it can be most harmful. SHADE is a desk organizer that is designed to help you curb your smartphone addiction and focus on work. Smartphones have areas when they can be useful, but while working it can be fatal to your productivity. The desk squire is meant to give your phone a home away from your pocket, blocking the screen while still being readily accessible.
